Our Mission


Our mission is to tackle hate speech. We built this Project to reduce the uncontrolled spread of hate in social media by supervising growing online content. We used machine learning to make the computer recognize hate speech in order for you to assess whether hate speech is in a particular text or not.  

Hate Speech

According to United Nation Strategy and Plan of Action on Hate Speech defines hate speech as…“any kind of communication in speech, writing or behaviour, that attacks or uses pejorative or discriminatory language with reference to a person or a group on the basis of who they are, in other words, based on their religion, ethnicity, nationality, race, colour, descent, gender or other identity factor.”

Why Hate Speech Detection

Hatify is the hate speech detection tool which identify and detect offensive or threatening speech being poured on the internet. Social media is the place for many people to make hateful comments against a particular group, especially on the basis of race, religion, or sexual orientation. Our Hate Speech Detector will be able to find out whether or not your text has hate speech.
